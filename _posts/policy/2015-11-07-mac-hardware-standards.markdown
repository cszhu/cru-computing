<h3>Mac Desktop</h3>
<p><b>Apple iMac</b></p>
<ul>
	<li>Processor: 2.7Ghz Quad-core i5 processor</li>
	<li>Memory: 8GB 1600MHz DDR3 Memory</li>
	<li>Storage: 1TB Serial ATA Drive @5400rpm</li>
</ul>
